Microplastic Pollution Risk Predictor

This platform analyzes environmental indicators such as population density, plastic waste production, recycling rate, rainfall and proximity to rivers to estimate the risk of microplastic contamination.

Global Plastic Waste

More than 350 million tons of plastic waste are produced each year globally.

Ocean Pollution

Between 1 and 2 million tons of plastic enter oceans every year.

Microplastic Definition

Microplastics are plastic particles smaller than 5mm found in oceans and rivers.

Understanding Microplastic Pollution

Microplastics originate from larger plastic waste that breaks down due to sunlight and environmental degradation.

Sources

Plastic packaging, clothing fibers and tire wear.

Environmental Impact

Microplastics harm marine ecosystems and biodiversity.

Human Health Risk

Microplastics may enter the human body through food and water.
